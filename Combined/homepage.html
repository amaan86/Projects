What We Do

Our vision includes sowing the seeds of positive change in the society which would further lead to the overall growth of the nation, to keep our morals and ethics alive and thereby not letting the humanity die and to uplift the unprivileged thereby maintaining equality and harmony in the society. The major target of Prinkit and his team is to provide education in slum areas. Education being a necessity to lead a fruitful life they identified the problem areas and started taking up projects.
